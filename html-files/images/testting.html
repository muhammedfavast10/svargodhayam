Welcome to Svargodayam!
Svargodayam is our college's premier arts festival, dedicated to celebrating the incredible talent and creativity of our students. This exciting event features a variety of performances and exhibitions, including music, dance, drama, and visual arts.

Join us as we highlight the artistic achievements of our community, fostering a sense of unity and pride. Stay updated with event results and support our talented participants.
Thank you for being part of Svargodayam. Let's celebrate the arts together!
